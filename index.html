20-22 December 2024

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, National Institute of Technology, Hamirpur is excited to announce its upcoming International Conference on International Conference on Artificial Intelligence, Device Computing, Communication, and Signal Processing(AIDCCSP-2024) on 20th and 22nd of December 2023 at National Institute of Technology, Hamirpur, India. This event will bring together researchers, experts, and practitioners from different fields to share their knowledge, insights, and experiences on the latest advancements and innovations in these domains. The conference aims to provide a platform for interdisciplinary collaborations and foster a vibrant community of researchers and practitioners in these areas. It will feature keynote speakers, technical sessions, and panel discussions, covering a broad range of topics related to machine learning, image processing, network security, and data sciences. We welcome experts from academia, industry, and government to join us in this conference. The event will provide an excellent opportunity for participants to learn from each other, network, and collaborate on new research projects. We look forward to your participation in this exciting event, and we hope that you will find the conference informative and thought-provoking.

National Institute of Technology, Hamirpur
National Institute of Technology Hamirpur is one of the thirty NITs in the country, established in 1986 as Regional Engineering College, as a joint and cooperative enterprise of the Govt. of India and Govt. of Himachal Pradesh. The goals of the institute as embodied in the logo are truly remarkable in their scope of vision. The Institute provides undergraduate, postgraduate and doctorate programmes in Engineering, Sciences & Humanities; fostering the spirit of national integration among the students, a close interaction with industry and a strong emphasis on research, both basic and applied.
